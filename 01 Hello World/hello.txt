Hello.c activities

1. Modify the program so it says hello to you instead of the world.
 
2. Add another line that says hello to someone sitting near you. Add a single line comment explaining what you are doing.

3. What does the \n sequence do? How could you test to find out?

4. What would \t do? 

5. What happens when you add the line display_clear(); ?

6. Remove the semicolon ; after any one statement. What happens when you try to compile? How could the messages you receive help you?
